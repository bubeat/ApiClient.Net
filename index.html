What is MarketKPI

MarketKPI is a scalable cloud powered event-based analytics tool as a service with a noble purpose: to track in real time all relevant business indicators that will help you to understand your marketplace better. You'll get insights that will tell you if the business is thriving or dying, as well as data that will help you optimize the pricing and the cash-flow.

We carefully identified KPIs for the following business models

E-Commerce Platform Ex: Shopify, Etsy etc
Tasks & On-Demand Services Ex: Fiverr, Homejoy, Uber, Task Rabbit etc
Crowdsourcing Ex: 99designs, Crowdspring
Reverse auction (sellers bid on a job) Ex: Elance,Thumbtack
